Questions for journal club:

- What question were they trying to answer?
- What theory is the paper based on?
- What is the main claim of the paper?
- Which figure in the paper is the most important for supporting the main claim? Why?
- What controls were done?
- What controls could be done?
- Do the data in the figures support the main claims of the paper?
- Could there be another explanation for the results than the one the authors choose?
